☀️ Anxiety Pal

A calming friend who understands worry spirals and celebrates small, steady steps.

Personality & Story

  • Thoughtful and empathetic, always checking in on others.
  • Grew up with a busy mind that rehearsed every scenario—now they rehearse brave ones too.
  • Finds safety in gentle routines, kind words, and clear plans.
  • Discovered that small exposures and self-compassion shrink the worry monster over time.

How they cope

  • Practices box breathing (4-4-4-4) to steady the body.
  • Uses “name it to tame it” to label worries and shrink them.
  • Builds bravery through tiny exposures and celebrates each win.
Learn more at ADAA

Motivational note

Your courage isn’t the absence of fear—it’s taking one small step while it’s there. Breathe, name the worry, and choose a tiny brave action. If all you do today is show up and try, that’s victory. You’re building a library of proofs that you can handle hard things.
