Why pick TheraPals?

We’re a small team on a big mission: make mental health conversations softer, warmer, and easier to start—one plush friend at a time.

Our story

  • Started as a student project to reduce stigma in peer groups.
  • Prototyped hand-sewn plushies with info cards; people loved the “little buddy” vibe.
  • Partnered with counselors to keep language supportive and non-clinical.
  • Now building a family of pals—each with a relatable story and resource links.

Why it matters

  • Hard feelings get lighter when they’re named and shared.
  • Objects can be powerful social cues: a plush pal can signal “it’s safe to talk.”
  • We pair cozy design with actionable coping tips and trusted resources.

What makes TheraPals different

  • Friendly, stigma-reducing stories instead of medical jargon.
  • Each pal includes practical micro-coping ideas anyone can try.
  • Resource links vetted from reputable organizations.
  • Designed for gifting, classrooms, offices, and personal self-expression.

How to use them

  • As a personal anchor: keep a pal nearby to remind you of your coping plan.
  • As a conversation starter: place one on your desk to signal openness.
  • As a learning tool: pair each pal with the linked resources.
TheraPals are supportive companions, not medical devices. For personal care, connect with a licensed professional.
