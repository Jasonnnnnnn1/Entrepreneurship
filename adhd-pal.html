⚡ ADHD — “Ziggy”

Hey! I’m Ziggy. My brain loves to sprint, not stroll, and sometimes it zips faster than I can keep up with.

Personality & Story

  • Curious, creative, and quick with ideas—brain like a rocket full of sparks.
  • Jumps between tasks, loses track, but always returns with fresh excitement.
  • Found confidence by turning “too much energy” into playful projects and movement breaks.
  • Learns best with visuals, timers, and friends who celebrate progress over perfection.

How they cope

  • Uses timers and colorful checklists to stay on track.
  • Takes movement breaks to reset energy.
  • Practices “one thing at a time” breathing to settle racing thoughts.
Learn more at CHADD

Motivational note

Hey, fellow spark! Your fast brain isn’t a flaw—it’s a gift. You notice patterns others miss. Chunk big tasks into tiny steps, set a timer, and move your body when you stall. Celebrate every checkbox, not just the finish line. You’re allowed to work with your brain, not against it.
